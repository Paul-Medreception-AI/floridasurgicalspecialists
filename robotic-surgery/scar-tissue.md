# Robotic Surgery for Scar Tissue

Scar tissue can limit visibility and motion. Robotic systems provide stability and dexterity to carefully separate adhesions and protect healthy structures.

## Advantages

3D vision and wristed tools improve safety when working through adhesions.

## Procedures affected

Hernia repairs, repeat gallbladder or colon surgery, and complex reconstructions often benefit.
