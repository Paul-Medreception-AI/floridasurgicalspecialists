# Laparoscopic Cholecystectomy

Laparoscopic cholecystectomy is the standard minimally invasive technique for gallbladder removal. It uses small incisions to remove the gallbladder safely and efficiently.

## Benefits of laparoscopy
Smaller incisions lead to less pain, shorter recovery, and faster return to normal activity. Most patients go home the same day.

## Who is a candidate
Most patients with gallstones, cholecystitis, or gallbladder dysfunction are candidates. We evaluate your health history to determine the best approach.
