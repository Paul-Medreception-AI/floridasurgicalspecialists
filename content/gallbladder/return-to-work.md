# Return to Work

Most patients return to work quickly after gallbladder surgery, especially with minimally invasive techniques. Timing depends on the physical demands of your job.

## Desk jobs vs physical work
Many patients with desk jobs return within a week. Jobs that involve lifting or physical labor may require more time for safe healing.
