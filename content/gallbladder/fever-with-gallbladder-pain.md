# Fever with Gallbladder Pain

Fever combined with right upper abdominal pain can indicate infection or acute inflammation of the gallbladder. This combination should be evaluated promptly.

## Why fever is concerning
Fever suggests an inflammatory or infectious process such as acute cholecystitis or bile duct infection. These conditions can worsen quickly without treatment.

## Evaluation and testing
We assess pain severity, check labs for infection, and perform imaging to confirm gallbladder disease. Rapid diagnosis helps prevent complications.

## Treatment
Antibiotics may be needed initially, but definitive treatment often requires gallbladder removal. We offer timely scheduling options and robotic expertise when appropriate.
