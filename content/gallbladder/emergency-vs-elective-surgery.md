# Emergency vs Elective Surgery

Gallbladder problems can require either planned surgery or urgent intervention. Understanding the difference helps you seek care at the right time.

## Elective surgery
Elective gallbladder surgery is planned after evaluation for recurring symptoms or confirmed stones. It allows time for preparation and scheduling.

## Emergency surgery
Emergency surgery may be needed for severe inflammation, infection, or bile duct obstruction. These situations can be dangerous without prompt care.
