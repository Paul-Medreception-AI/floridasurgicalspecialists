# Recovery Timeline

Recovery after gallbladder removal is usually straightforward, especially with minimally invasive techniques. Most patients feel significantly better within days.

## Typical milestones
Many patients walk the same day, return to light activity within a few days, and resume normal routines in one to two weeks. Full recovery varies by individual and job demands.

## What to watch for
Mild soreness and fatigue are common early on. We provide guidance on incision care, pain control, and signs that warrant a call.
