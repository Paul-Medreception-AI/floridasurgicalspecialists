# When Gallbladder Pain Is Dangerous

Gallbladder pain is not always an emergency, but certain symptoms signal higher risk. Knowing the warning signs can protect your health.

## Red flags
Severe, persistent pain, fever, jaundice, or vomiting are signs of possible infection or bile duct blockage. These symptoms require urgent evaluation.

## Why prompt care matters
Delaying treatment can lead to complications such as gallbladder rupture or pancreatitis. Rapid assessment helps prevent escalation.
