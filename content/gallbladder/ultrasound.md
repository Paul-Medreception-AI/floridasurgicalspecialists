# Gallbladder Ultrasound

Ultrasound is the most common test for gallbladder disease. It is fast, noninvasive, and excellent at detecting gallstones or gallbladder wall thickening.

## What ultrasound can show
An ultrasound can identify stones, sludge, swelling, and signs of inflammation. It also evaluates bile ducts to look for blockage.

## When ultrasound is recommended
If you have right upper quadrant pain, nausea after meals, or biliary colic, ultrasound is often the first diagnostic step.
