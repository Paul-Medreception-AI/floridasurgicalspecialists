# ER vs Surgeon

Knowing when to visit the emergency room versus scheduling a surgical consultation can save time and reduce risk.

## When the ER is appropriate
Go to the ER for severe pain, fever, jaundice, or persistent vomiting. These signs can indicate infection or obstruction that needs immediate care.

## When to see a surgeon
For recurring pain after meals or known gallstones, a surgical consultation is appropriate. We can evaluate and plan elective surgery to prevent emergencies.
