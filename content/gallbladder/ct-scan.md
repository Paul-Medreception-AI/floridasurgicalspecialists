# CT Scan

A CT scan provides detailed cross-sectional images of the abdomen and can detect gallbladder inflammation, complications, or other causes of pain.

## When CT is used
CT is helpful if symptoms are severe, if complications are suspected, or if ultrasound results are inconclusive. It can also evaluate the pancreas and bile ducts.

## What CT can show
CT can detect gallbladder wall thickening, fluid, abscess, or signs of bile duct obstruction. It also helps assess other abdominal conditions.
