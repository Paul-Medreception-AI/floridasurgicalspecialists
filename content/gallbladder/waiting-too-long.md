# Waiting Too Long

Delaying treatment for gallbladder disease can increase the risk of emergencies. Recurrent attacks can lead to infection, obstruction, or pancreatitis.

## Risks of waiting
Repeated inflammation can scar the gallbladder and make surgery more complex. It can also result in ER visits and longer recovery.

## Benefits of timely care
Early surgical care often means minimally invasive options, faster recovery, and fewer complications. We prioritize timely access when appropriate.
