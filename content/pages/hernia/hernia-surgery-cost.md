## What affects the cost of hernia surgery
Costs vary based on:
- Hernia type (inguinal vs ventral/incisional vs hiatal evaluation)
- Complexity (size, recurrence, scar tissue, need for reconstruction)
- Surgical approach (open vs laparoscopic vs robotic)
- Facility, anesthesia, and perioperative needs
- Insurance coverage, deductibles, and co-insurance
