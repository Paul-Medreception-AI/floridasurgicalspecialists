## Sarah Assali, D.O.

Dr. Sarah Assali was born and raised in Allentown, PA. She obtained her medical degree from Lake Erie College of Osteopathic Medicine in 2018. She went on to complete her general surgery residency at Allegheny Health Network in Pittsburgh PA. During this time, she developed a keen interest in minimally invasive surgery, going on to pursue additional training in laparoscopic and robotic techniques to enhance her skillset. In addition to her career interests, Dr. Assali enjoys camping, hiking, traveling and spending time with her friends and family.

## Casey Joe, M.D.

Dr. Casey Joe is from New York area who received Bachelor's degree in Toxicology at John Jay College of Criminal Justice in New York. She then pursued her passion in medicine and received medical degree from American University of the Caribbean School of Medicine. She completed general surgery residency at BronxCare Health System in New York and joined Florida Surgical Specialists to pursue her further interests in minimally invasive surgery.  
Dr. Joe, during her free time, enjoys hiking, bicycling, and traveling.
